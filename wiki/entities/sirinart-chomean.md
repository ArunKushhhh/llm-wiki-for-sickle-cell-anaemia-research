# Sirinart Chomean

## Research Focus

Chomean's lab focuses on developing molecular diagnostic tools for hemoglobinopathies — particularly α-thalassemia — with emphasis on biosensor platforms that can reach clinical and resource-constrained settings.

## Research Trajectory

Chomean's work shows a clear progression:
1. **2010:** Proof-of-concept biosensor for SEA deletion (QCM) — required probe immobilization
2. **2018:** Isothermal amplification (LAMP) — moving toward field applicability
3. **2022:** EIS generalized to other human gene targets (HLA genotyping)
4. **~2023:** Full integration of mPCR + SPCE EIS for clinical SEA detection — highest impact study, 81-sample validation

> Chomean is the leading group working on exactly the sensor platform relevant to this research. Future papers from this lab should be prioritized for ingestion.
