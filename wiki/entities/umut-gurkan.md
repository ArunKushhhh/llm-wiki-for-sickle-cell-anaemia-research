## Research Focus

Gurkan's lab develops microfluidic and biosensing platforms for hematological disorders, with a strong focus on **sickle cell disease (SCD)** functional assays and point-of-care diagnostics.

NSF CAREER, NHLBI (multiple grants), Cure Sickle Cell Initiative, American Heart Association. Strong presence in the NIH Cure Sickle Cell Initiative ecosystem.

> [!NOTE] Relevance to research focus
> Gurkan's group is a leading lab for functional microfluidic assessment of SCD RBCs. Their impedance-based REI is the first validated, imaging-free electrical biomarker for SCD RBC deformability. Future papers from this lab (especially on deoxygenated testing, POC adaptation, and thalassemia extension) should be prioritized for ingestion.
